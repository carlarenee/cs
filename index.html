Hi, I'm Carla, thanks for stopping by!

I'm a multidisciplinary web leader combining 15+ years of creative and technical experience across design, development, and product delivery. I co‑founded and ran a small business for ten years, owning brand strategy, web and newsletter production, and multi‑channel marketing, before transitioning into web and software development.

I spent the last 9 years as a full stack developer, technical lead and web development manager for the 3rd largest US book publisher. My roles have included overseeing developer teams, both US based and offshore, managing product roadmaps, heading UX research and portfolios of WordPress and Shopify sites, stakeholder collaboration, as well as user management and training.

These days, I'm working on an app, consulting for small businesses to find their best digital strategy and trying to be a gardener. When I'm not working on websites, I'm out in the garden, on the slopes or at the beach! I love being outdoors, getting lost in nature.

Please look through some of my past projects and let me know if you have any questions or interest in collaborating. I would love to hear about your latest project or idea... or gardening tips!
